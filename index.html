Black Lotus

  Black Lotus Cooperative is all about rising up out of figurative murky water into something beautiful through and through. People have trials of tribulations that need support. This cooperative is here to build our individuals up from the hands of others through resources and exchange. Membership with BLC allows you to build a stronger network, a family from afar; our members are all across the US.

Our Core Values

Integrity

   We conduct business in a way that is honest, transparent and ethical. We provide financial assistance that will empower clientele to take control over their finances with direct pay . 

Empower

   We seek to do more than provide a quick fix for an individual situation; we strive to fix larger systemic problems and empower our client base to live a better life with programs that will support the mind and body on more than an individual basis. Children are the future. Through funding we aim to develop programs focused towards empowering the youth.  

Embracing National Community 

   We collaborate with other agencies and partners in our communities from state to state. We believe that we can accomplish more together if we are able to build resourceful alliances.

Positive Donation Stewardship

   While money is a fun commodity to share so is time, knowledge, experience. We value positive stewardship from the essence of our donors into the heart and hands of others. Not one person can ever run out of that worthwhile to give.
